iGA Spotter

Trkiye's first official Spotter Area, for aviation photography enthusiasts, has been launched at Istanbul Airport. The Spotter Area at Istanbul Airport is strategically positioned, with a prime view of Runway 1 and the Air Traffic Control Tower. Applications to access the iGA Spotter Area, highly sought-after by aviation enthusiasts, are free of charge.

Applications for events in 2024 will start on April.
Applications will be accepted from 24 October 2023 - 01 November 2023. Please submit your application between these dates.
