Contact Information and Services for Disabled Passengers

 

Wheelchair users can make use of the accessible Havaist midibuses. To avoid extended waiting times, guests wishing to use this service are advised to notify us in advance.

 

Wheelchair users traveling to the city center from the airport, are advised to submit their requests at the Havaist counters located on the transportation floor, at least 1 hour in advance, specifying their preferred route.

 

Wheelchair users traveling to the airport from the city center, are advised to submit their requests at least 6 hours in advance through the Havaist communication channels, specifying their preferred route and pick-up point.

The (M11) Gayrettepe - Istanbul Airport Metro commenced operations between Kağıthane and İstanbul Havalimanı on January 22, 2023, and stops at 8 stations. With the addition of the Gayrettepe line on January 29, 2024, it now serves eight stations.

The Istanbul Airport metro line features 8 stations: Gayrettepe, Kağıthane, Üniversite-Hasdal, Kemerburgaz, Göktürk, İhsaniye, İstanbul Havalimanı, and Kargo Terminal.

The Istanbul Airport metro line was built by the Ministry of Transport and Infrastructure and is managed by the TCDD.

The travel time between the Gayrettepe and İstanbul Havalimanı stations on the Istanbul Airport metro line is 30 minutes.
