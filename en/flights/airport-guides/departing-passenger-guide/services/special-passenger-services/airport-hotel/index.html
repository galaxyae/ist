Airport Hotel

YOTEL Istanbul Airport Hotel

YOTEL Istanbul Airport is one of the biggest airport hotels in the world and the largest in Europe, boasting a total of 451 cabins (rooms), with 171 on the landside and 280 on the airside. While anyone can book accommodation on the landside of YOTEL, only individuals traveling internationally can book accommodation on the airside at YOTELAIR.

YOTEL Istanbul Airport welcomes passengers with two hotels, one landside and the other airside. YOTEL has been thoughtfully designed to cater not only to passengers traveling for business or leisure, but also to flight crews at Istanbul Airport and the wider corporate community in Istanbul. Featuring state-of-the-art cabins, ergonomically adjustable, custom-made SmartBeds™, smart TVs compatible with guests' mobile devices for personalized content viewing, dynamic ambient lighting, monsoon rain showers, complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi, and attentive staff, our establishment provides comfortable and convenient accommodation.

In addition to meeting and resting facilities in the communal areas, we offer self-service food and beverage options in the airside Komyuniti Lounge section. Guests staying on the landside can also take advantage of YOTEL Istanbul Airport's 24-hour fitness center and the KOMYUNITI Bar and Restaurant.

 

With its proximity to the departures floor and passport control points, and offering cabin bookings for short stays starting from 4 hours, YOTEL Istanbul Airport is the perfect choice for guests traveling on long-haul flights. Also known as YOTEL Istanbul, the hotel offers various-sized meeting rooms, equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, for companies and business travelers.

Recharge Cabins

Perfect for a quick pit-stop, YOTELAIR’s Recharge Cabins provide travelers with a space to stop, snooze, and go. Cabins can be booked by the hour and offer a bed, TV, and desk, along with access to shared shower and bathroom facilities. Located opposite the entrance to the iGA Lounge, follow the signs for YOTELAIR and take the escalator one floor up to find the entrance to the Recharge Cabins. Available for walk-in bookings only.
